Airline Premium Cabin Gallery

This project is my brainchild! I currently work for a luxury travel agency which mainly sells discounted business class and first class airline tickets. As part of our presentation we send our clients images of business class or first class cabins of the airline that they are potentially booking with however since most airlines are inconsistent w/ their premium cabin configuration, googling the right image of the product is sometimes time-consuming which inevitably delays transactions. To save me and my colleagues time I have decided to create this project. The gallery provides a more organized and updated airline cabin images with product descriptions so that with just a few clicks my colleagues and I are able to create product presentation to our clients in no time.

Created w/ HTML, CSS AND JS and a lot googling.
